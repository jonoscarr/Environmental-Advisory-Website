2. Reduce, Reuse and Recycle

Practicing the principles of reduce, reuse, and recycle is crucial for minimizing our environmental impact. By reducing consumption, we limit waste and conserve resources. Reusing items extends their life cycle and reduces the need for new products. Recycling materials ensures they are processed and used again, decreasing landfill waste and conserving natural resources.
